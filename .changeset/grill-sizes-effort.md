Make **`grill-with-docs`** and **`grill-me`** size the effort as they open, and hand off to **`wayfinder`** when it's too big for one session.

Both now judge, at the start of the grill, whether the idea plausibly fits one focused session. If instead it's a huge, foggy effort where the way to the destination isn't even visible yet, they **stop and recommend `/wayfinder`** — which charts that fog as a multi-session map of decisions — rather than grinding on a degraded session. They **recommend, never invoke**: both grills and wayfinder are user-invoked, and entering a multi-session map should be the human's deliberate call (grill-me also notes wayfinder wants a repo/tracker to hold its map).

`ask-tutu` now frames `grill-with-docs` as the default door that escalates to `wayfinder`, and the docs pages for grill-with-docs, grill-me, and wayfinder describe the sizing hand-off.
