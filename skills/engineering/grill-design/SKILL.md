Run a `/grilling` session, using the `/design-taste` skill.

Grill the visual and interaction design, not the domain: walk the three dials (variance, motion, density), the references, and the type/colour/motion tokens one decision at a time, recommending an answer for each. As each choice crystallises, capture it in `DESIGN.md` inline — the visual counterpart to what `/grill-with-docs` does for `CONTEXT.md`.
