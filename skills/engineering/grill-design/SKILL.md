Run a `/grilling` session, using the `/design-taste` skill.

Grill the visual and interaction design, not the domain: walk the three dials (variance, motion, density), the references, and the type/colour/motion tokens one decision at a time, recommending an answer for each. As each choice crystallises, capture it in `DESIGN.md` inline — the visual counterpart to what `/grill-with-docs` does for `CONTEXT.md`.

One of the decisions to settle and record is the **design flow** — `hi-fi-only` (the [open-design](../open-design/SKILL.md) reference is the sole design artifact) or `mockup+hi-fi` (a low-fi [mockup](../mockup/SKILL.md) for structure plus the reference for the look). Write it as a `Design flow:` line in `DESIGN.md`: `/open-design`, `/mockup`, `/pre-flight`, `/implement`, and the design eval all read it to know which artifacts to expect, so settling it here keeps the rest of the flow from guessing.
